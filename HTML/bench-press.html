Bench Press

Type: Compound Exercise

Movement: Horizontal Push

Equipment: Barbell, Flat Bench

Target Muscle Group

Primary: Pectoralis Major (Mid Chest)

Secondary: Triceps, Anterior Deltoids (Front Shoulders)

Recommended Reps & Sets

  • For Muscle Growth: 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps
  • For Strength: 4-6 sets of 3-6 reps
  • For Endurance: 2-3 sets of 12-20 reps

Key Benefits

  • Builds upper body strength
  • Enhances chest muscle size and definition
  • Engages multiple muscle groups for efficient training
